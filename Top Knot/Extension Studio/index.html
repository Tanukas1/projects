Hair Extension Services

Our hair extension services are tailored to your individual needs and preferences. Hair extensions are an excellent way to add length, volume, and thickness to your hair. Hair extensions come in a variety of types, including clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, and fusion. Clip-in hair extensions are the most popular type, made of hair wefts with clips attached that you can clip into your natural hair. Tape-in hair extensions involve attaching wefts of hair to your natural hair using a tape adhesive. Sew-in hair extensions involve attaching wefts of hair to your natural hair using a needle and thread. Fusion hair extensions involve attaching individual strands of hair to your natural hair using a heat tool.

Hair extensions come in a variety of colors, lengths, and textures, making it easy to find the perfect match for your hair. You can choose from natural human hair extensions or synthetic hair extensions, depending on your budget and preference. Hair extensions can last up to six to eight weeks with proper care and maintenance, making them a great option for special events or everyday wear.
